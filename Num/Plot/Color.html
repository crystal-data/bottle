    enum Num::Plot::Color

    Overview

    Sets the color index for cmap0 (see the section called “Color Map0”).

    0 black (default background) 1 red (default foreground) 2 yellow 3 green 4 aquamarine 5 pink 6 wheat 7 grey 8 brown 9 blue 10 BlueViolet 11 cyan 12 turquoise 13 magenta 14 salmon 15 white Use plscmap0 to change the entire cmap0 color palette and plscol0 to change an individual color in the cmap0 color palette.

    Defined in:

    plot/internal/palette.cr
